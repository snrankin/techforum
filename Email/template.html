Good afternoon!

On behalf of the Tech Forum committee, I am excited to present this years schedule with amazing lineup of guest speakers and presenters from Arizona, nationwide and 2 international guests. We look so forward to having you on campus for our annual Tech Forum and are so thankful you are willing to give back. You knowledge and experience will truly help our students as they pursue their passions in technology and we cannot thank you enough for your insight during their education!!

The Tech Forum website will connect you to the official speaker schedule, as well as information about our leading tech pros. You can also see past sessions and see the venue space you'll be speaking in. The theatre space holds around 100, and depending on the topic there can be anywhere from 20 students to a packed house. To offer some perspective on expected crowd size; since we offer a variety of majors here at UAT some of our smaller majors may not have as many students in attendance, however 20 people may be 100% of the entire major and actually be a great turnout in which the students are so thankful to have you there. We also livestream every presentation so that our online student population is able to attend as well. There are usually anywhere between 5-50 people online listening as well. You will get access to your presentation afterward and are welcome to share, post to your blog/website as well.

While this tech-centric event is purposed for our students here at UAT, it is also open to the community as well. Please feel free to pass along this schedule to your coworks and feel free to share with prospective students and the community as they are welcome to attend.

* On the schedule you will note there is time set aside on Friday at 2:30pm for portfolio reviews. We ask our speakers to give their professional opinion to our students that are looking for some one on one feedback when it comes to their portfolios they are creating for graduation. If you are willing to set aside that hour while on campus to help our students please let me know and we will schedule you 2-3 students to personally help in your field of expertise.

If you are traveling from out of state, look for travel information for hotel confirmation, transportation and meal information next week. It is all in process and will be coming to you shortly.

We are excited to have such a diverse group of speakers for this Fall Tech Forum and look forward to welcoming all of you to campus!

Please let me know if you have any questions. Thanks so much for you time and we will see you oh-so-soon on campus!
